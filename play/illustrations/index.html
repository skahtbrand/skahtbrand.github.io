Illustrations

To keep my creative juices flowing over the winter months I taught myself how to illustrate. I purchased a Wacom tablet and have been drawing cars and other things for many years. Previously, I had only used Adobe Illustrator to design logos and icons for web applications. Drawing a car takes a fair amount of time but it’s a great way to pass the time on a snowy winter day.

Learning Illustrator has also given me a way to design t-shirts for myself and others. Why wear someone else’s design when you can design your own. I currently use Redbubble.com to sell my designs on apparel.
