Shop

Over the years I have tried and failed over and over trying to come up with something people would buy on a shirt. I have done designs for others in the automotive industry with success but most do not know it was me. As my time as a photographer a lot of it was for skootermedia to promote myself and no one wanted that.

After I closed down skootermedia I came up with a new brand name Signal. I tried a few things with that but no one knew what that was either.

Then I purchased my Porsche and I started coming up with ideas that maybe other Porsche people would want. A few months back I came up with my “Key Goes On The Left” design and it has been my best selling design to date.

So you really never know what will be a hit but you have to keep trying.
